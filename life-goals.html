Overview

Replication protocol

Replications in this block must follow the shared protocol to ensure comparability across cohorts.

Required variables

  • Open-ended life goal responses
  • Coding of leadership-related goals (using the shared coding scheme)
  • Gender, age, education, employment status

Required analyses

  • Test for gender differences in the likelihood of listing leadership-related goals
  • Report effect sizes (e.g., odds ratios, Cohen’s d, or regression coefficients)

Exploratory add-ons

Student teams may add extra variables (e.g., self-determination, leadership self-efficacy, social identity) and exploratory analyses, as long as these are clearly labelled and documented on OSF.
